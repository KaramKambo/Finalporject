Welcome to Africa!

Notable Locations

What are some notable locations in Africa? Well, given that there are so many countries, here is a brief rundown.

Zanzibar, known for its beautiful beaches, is a fantastic place to visit in Africa.It is often a quiet and calming destination and allows for relaxation. Victoria Falls, known for its breathtaking view, is between Zambia and Zimbabwe. It's almost twice as deep as the Niagara Falls, and it's an incredible sight. Tanzania, known for it's iconic safari, is a continental treasure. It's filled with various animals and often a tourist destination. Serengeti National Park, known for the up-close interactions with many animals, is also a very well-known African safari. Cairo, known for its bustling city vibe, has so much to offer. Everywhere you turn is something worth seeing, with the Pyramids of Giza to the Nile River. Mauritius, known for its subtle yet gorgeousness, is a large appeal to tourists because of it's beauty and general experience. There are many luxurious resorts as well as great architecture and cuisine. Cape Town, known for its many hiking trails, is a constant for tourists. There is a lot to sightsee with dramatic scenery and remarkable beaches. Kruger National Park is known for its occupation with many animals. It's very popular. Masai Mara National Reserve is known for its home to many wild beasts, including cheetahs, elephants, lions, wildebeests, hippos and many more. It's a stunning safari that has a lot to offer. Seychelles is a archipelago of fifteen islands in the Indian Ocean, a very relaxing and tranquil environment. Marrakech is a historic Moroccan city, filled with spices, textiles, and architecture from the past. Madagascar is known for its name as well as it's pristine condition; the landscape is unspoiled and many animals are visible to visitors. Malawi is a rather small location in Africa but is nevertheless gorgeous because of its landscape, diverse wildlife,and many amiable people. Botswana is known for its naturesque environment, is filled with animals, and is a very adventurous visit. Luxor is a staple because while in Egypt, it is still very much so connected to Africa, and is never overlooked. It has historical precedent and is always a fun visit.
