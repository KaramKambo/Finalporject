Notable Locations

What are some notable locations in South America? Here is a brief rundown.

Firstly, we can start out with Brazil, which is an extremely hot tourist destination for people to visit, especially in the summer. Rio De Janiero has a famous Christ the Redeemer Statue, which millions of people flock to each year. Additionally, the beaches in Brazil are famous for water activities and there is a giant party held on New Years Eve every year on Copacabana beach. It is also popular to go to Argentina and Chile for soccer matches and watching the world cup. Another infamous destination is Machu Picchu in Peru, which is known for its gorgeous scenery and llamas roaming widely. There is a large hike up the mountains to get to the ancient civilization and there are tour groups leading into the ruins.
