FAQS

How do I know this website is legitimate?

We use the most reliable websites to gather the most reliable information.

  • Your safety, security, and comfort is of utmost importance to us.
  • We do our best to stay consistent with the times and make sure we are delivering and posting accurate information.
  • We appreciate your support and would greatly appreciate feedback.
  • We are constantly trying to improve and make changes to our site that make it both easier to understand and more interactive.

Will my information be shared?

No. We do not gather information. There is no required account information, so you do not have to sign in or sign up.

Can I connect to flights?

At the moment, you cannot. We are in the process of developing a way for that to be possible, however. We apologize for any inconveniences.
