Notable Locations

What are some notable locations in Europe? Here is a brief rundown.

Starting out with England, there are many famous sights in London and the countryside. For example, there is the Big Ben tower in London, the London eye, which is a famous ferris wheel, and Stonehenge, a historical formation of rocks. Moving onto France, the Eiffel tower is one of the most popular tourist destinations, the Louvre is a famous art museum for historical European paintings, and the South of France is known for its amazing branch. Next is Italy, where there are multiple fun cities and things to explore. In Rome, there is the Colosseum, Florence has the duomo; and Pisa houses the infamous Tower of Pisa. Spain has the Sagrada Familia in Barcelona and other tourist locations in Madrid. Barcelona has very famous beaches, known for their synthetic sand and breathtaking water. There is also other countries in Europe such as Greece and Turkey, which have notable locations like the Hagia Sofia and the popular Greek island of Santorini. Lastly, Germany and Austria are countries in middle Europe with many tourist destinations and popular festivals.
